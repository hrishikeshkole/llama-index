Vector databases are specialized databases designed to store and query high-dimensional vectors.
In the context of AI and machine learning, text documents, images, and other forms of unstructured data are converted into numerical representations called embeddings.
A vector database allows for efficient similarity searches, finding data points that are conceptually similar to a given query.
Popular vector databases include ChromaDB, Pinecone, Milvus, Qdrant, and Weaviate.
These databases are a critical component of modern AI applications, especially RAG pipelines.
